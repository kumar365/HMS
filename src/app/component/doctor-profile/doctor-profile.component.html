About Me

Dr. Ganesh K is an expert and experienced Orthopedician with an experience of 22 years, and specialize in Joint Replacement Surgeon. He graduated and obtained his MBBS degree from Madurai Medical College, Madurai in 1996, and then his po stgraduate MS Degree from G.S.V.M Medical College, Kanpur in 2002. He is a Fellowship Trained Joint Replacement Surgeon and specializes in Primary and Revision Joint Replacement of hip, knee, shoulder and elbow. He has a special interest in Unicompartmental Knee Replacement and Computer Navigated Total Knee Replacement.
